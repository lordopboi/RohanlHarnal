@ROHANHARNAL

Rohan Harnal is a visionary photographer based in Delhi, India, with over nine years of experience in capturing moments through his lens. His journey began with a diploma in photojournalism and documentary at Aurobindo College of Arts and Communication, Delhi.

Expanding his horizons, Rohan pursued studio photography at SPEOS in Paris. His dual background equips him with a versatile approach, seamlessly transitioning from raw, candid moments to polished studio shoots.

Rohan Harnal's lens captures diverse clients like Rani Pink Gift, Sepoy & Co, Simba Beer, and DIVA Restaurants. His portfolio blends commercial prowess with a deep understanding of subjects, showcased in captivating model portfolios. Recognized for his talent, Rohan pushes artistic boundaries, creating images that transcend mere moments.
